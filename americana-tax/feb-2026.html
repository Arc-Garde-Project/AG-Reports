What Worked
Orange County outperformed LA County. OC delivered 7 leads at $43.80 CPL vs LA's 5 leads at $60.71 CPL. With nearly identical spend ($306 vs $303), OC is the more efficient market. The audience responds faster and converts at a lower cost.
Video ads crushed static images on engagement. Video CTR averaged 3.38% vs static's 1.65%. Video CPC was $1.91 vs static's $6.29. People engage with video content far more than static images for this audience. The format shift was the right call.
Static Image 2 dominated retargeting. It captured 57.8% of all retargeting spend across 6 weeks. The algorithm consistently chose it over the other 4 images. Static 4 was a distant second at 24.1%. The retargeting front-runner is clear.
Landing page conversion improved over time. LP conversion rate climbed to 9.09% in weeks 5-6, up from 2.8-5.7% in weeks 1-4. As the algorithm learned which audiences to target, the people landing on the page were more qualified and more likely to fill out the questionnaire.
What Needs Attention
Video hook rate is declining. Week 5 hook rate was 6.63%, week 6 dropped to 4.83%. Both are well below the 30% benchmark. The first 3 seconds of the video are not grabbing attention hard enough. The video creative needs a stronger opening hook.
CPC is trending up in week 6. Video CPC went from $1.38 in week 5 to $3.08 in week 6. ThruPlays dropped from 81 to 44, while cost per ThruPlay nearly doubled ($1.06 to $1.96). This signals early creative fatigue on the video ads.
LA County is underperforming on CPL. LA's $60.71 CPL is 39% more expensive than OC's $43.80. Week 3 (LA W2) was the worst at $128.48 CPL with only 1 lead. LA needs either better targeting, different creative angles, or reduced budget allocation.
Recommended Next Steps
1. New video creative with a stronger hook. The current videos hold well (39.6% hold rate) and complete well (27.8%), but the opening 3 seconds are the weak link. Test a new opening: lead with the pain point or a bold claim in the first frame. The audience will stay if you can get them past the first 3 seconds.
2. Shift budget toward Orange County. OC delivers 39% cheaper leads than LA. Consider a 60/40 or 70/30 OC/LA budget split instead of the current 50/50. Let the more efficient market carry the campaign.
3. Kill underperforming retargeting creatives. Static 1, 3, and 5 combined only account for 18.1% of retargeting spend. Replace them with 3 new test images while keeping Static 2 (the top performer) and Static 4 (the secondary performer) running.
4. Stick with video for prospecting. The data is clear: video drives 2x the CTR and 3x lower CPC than static images. Even with the hook rate issue, video is the right format. Fix the creative, not the format.
5. Move into scaling phase. After 6 weeks of testing, the winning formula is identified: video prospecting in Orange County with Static 2 retargeting. The next phase should increase daily budget from $20/day to $35-50/day on the winning combination and monitor CPL closely for the first 7 days.
